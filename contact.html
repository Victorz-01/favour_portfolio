Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? Here are some answers to the most common ones I get.

What is your typical turnaround time?
Turnaround time depends on the project scope. Simple edits can be done in 2-3 days, while more complex projects like music videos or commercials may take 1-2 weeks. I always communicate timelines clearly before starting.
What video formats do you deliver?
I deliver in any format you need ‚Äî MP4, MOV, AVI, or platform-specific formats optimized for YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, etc. I can also provide different aspect ratios (16:9, 9:16, 1:1) as needed.
How many revisions do you offer?
I typically include 2-3 rounds of revisions with every project. I make sure to understand your vision clearly from the start so we get it right quickly. Additional revisions can be arranged if needed.
Do you work with clients outside Nigeria?
Absolutely! I work with clients globally. All communication and file transfers happen online, so location is never a barrier. We can communicate via WhatsApp, email, or video calls.
What do you need from me to get started?
To get started, I'll need your raw footage, any reference videos or mood boards, music preferences, and a brief description of what you're looking for. The more details you provide, the better the final result will be!
How do payments work?
I typically require a 50% deposit before starting work, with the remaining 50% due upon completion. Payment methods include bank transfer, and other options can be discussed. For international clients, I accept various payment platforms.
